## Q-4 resolution (frozen)

Applied the C-3a spec v2 percentages literally (S1=70%, S2=60%, S3=40%) — see
`data/seed/02-seed-incident/seed.conf`. Appendix B's arithmetic check predicted this would fall
short of the "−10..12%" headline when measured over the *calendar* H2 (Jul–Dec) window (predicted
−8.4%, actual −8.44% — exact) and offered three fixes: deeper deletion, a wider window, or
re-labeling the KPI to a narrower window. **Resolution: the third option, but it turns out exact,
not approximate** — the seed's own window (weeks 32–48) measures **−10.62%**, squarely inside the
spec'd range, because week-32–48 is narrower than calendar H2 and excludes weeks where Marketplace
is naturally higher-volume (peak season starts before wk 32 per F-2's seasonality). **Briefing
KPI: "Marketplace down 10.6% in the 17 weeks of the incident" (wks 32–48), not a calendar-H2
figure.** November alone is −12.45% (also exact vs the "≈−12%" target).
